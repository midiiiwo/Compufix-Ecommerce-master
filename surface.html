Microsoft Surface

Microsoft Surface laptops are a testament to innovation and sleek design, seamlessly combining power and portability for a cutting-edge computing experience. With a signature premium build, the Surface Laptop series boasts vibrant PixelSense displays, responsive keyboards, and precision trackpads, ensuring a comfortable and productive user interface. Powered by the latest Intel processors, these laptops deliver robust performance, making multitasking a breeze. The integration of Windows 10, coupled with a seamless pen experience, enhances creativity and productivity, while the elegant, minimalist design reflects Microsoft's commitment to both form and function. Whether it's the lightweight Surface Laptop Go or the high-performance Surface Laptop Studio, Microsoft's Surface lineup continues to redefine the laptop landscape with a perfect blend of style and substance.

Product Specification

  • Processor (CPU): Intel Core processors (i3, i5, i7, and possibly i9 depending on the model). AMD Ryzen processors in some models.
  • Graphics (GPU): Integrated Intel UHD Graphics for standard configurations. Dedicated graphics options from NVIDIA (such as GeForce or Quadro) in higher-end models. Some Latitude models may use integrated AMD Radeon graphics.
  • Memory (RAM): DDR4 RAM configurations ranging from 4GB to 32GB or more, depending on the model.
  • Storage: Solid State Drives (SSD) for faster performance and responsiveness. Hard Disk Drives (HDD) may be available in certain configurations for larger storage capacity.
  • Display: Various display sizes and resolutions, including Full HD (1080p) and higher. Some models may offer touchscreen functionality.
  • Connectivity: USB ports (Type-A and Type-C). HDMI or DisplayPort for external display connectivity. Ethernet port for wired networking. Wireless connectivity through Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.
  • Security Features: Fingerprint readers for biometric authentication. Smart card readers. Trusted Platform Module (TPM) for hardware-based security.
  • Battery: Lithium-ion batteries with varying capacities for different models. Some Latitude laptops may offer options for extended battery life configurations.
  • Build and Design: Robust construction with MIL-STD-810G certification for durability. Various form factors, including traditional clamshell designs and 2-in-1 convertibles.
